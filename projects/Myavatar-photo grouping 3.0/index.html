User Guide

Selecting Photos:
1. Click on individual photos to select.
2. To select all photos, click the "Select All" button.
Organizing Photos into Folders:
1. Press '1' to move selected photos to Folder 1.
2. Press '2' to move selected photos to Folder 2.
3. Press 'X' to move selected photos to Folder X.
Manipulating Photos:
1. Press 'A' or the left arrow key to shift selected photos one step left.
2. Press 'D' or the right arrow key to shift selected photos one step right.
Deleting and Deselecting Photos:
1. Press 'Q' or 'Backspace' to delete selected photos.
2. Press 'C' to deselect all photos.
